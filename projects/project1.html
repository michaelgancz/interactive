Andrea Mazzariello is a composer, performer and educator whose practice runs a fascinating gamut of genera, styles, aesthetics and techniques. I met him online at a composition festival last summer and was struck by his capacity for attentive listening and the genuine humanity of his online presence. In this experimental interview, Andrea was given just under two weeks to answer a set of five open-ended questions.
