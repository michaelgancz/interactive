The Story so far:

This website generates semi-random one-room dungeons for tabletop roleplaying games. The user, or dungeon master, begins by selecting a climate environment in which the dungeon is found. The dungeon master may then 'roll' a virtual die for a number of random objects and characeristics that can populate this dungeon. This process and its contents is loosely adapted from the Dungeons & Dragons 5th Edition Dungeon Master's Guide.
